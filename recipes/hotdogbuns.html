Chinese Hot Dog Buns

Description

These fluffy hot dog buns will blow your mind. They're soft, tender, rich, smooth, and super buttery.

The fluffy bread is wrapped around a hot dog for a satisfying snack. Perfect for on-the-go!

Ingredients

Tangzhong:

  • 25g bread flour
  • 125ml milk

For Bread:

  • 350g bread flour
  • 55g sugar
  • 3g salt
  • 7g yeast
  • 15g milk powder (optional)
  • 1 large egg (room temperature)
  • 125ml milk
  • 30g unsalted butter (room temperature)

Steps

  1. Make the tangzhong.
  2. Set aside and cool to room temperature.
  3. In bowl of mixer, mix bread flour, sugar, salt, yeast mixture (milk and yeast), and milk powder
  4. Add butter and mix for 5 mins on low speed.
  5. Increase speed to medium and mix for another 5mins.
  6. Total in mixer - 15 mins.
  7. Let dough sit for 1~ hour until dough doubles size.
  8. Deflate dough and separate into 10 pieces.
  9. Roll around hot dogs.
  10. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes
